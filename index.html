How we work

Your team sits in our sprints. Literally.

Two-week cycles, four ceremonies, no separate "internal-only" track. Knowledge transfer happens inside the work, not as a phase bolted on at the end.

Daily

Stand-up

15 minutes. Progress, blockers, next step — with client-side staff in the room when paired.

Sprint start

Planning

Stories estimated and selected together, so priority isn't handed down — it's reasoned through.

Sprint end

Review & demo

Live walkthroughs of finished work, including the decisions behind it — not just the output.

After review

Retrospective

One concrete improvement, every sprint. Documented, not just discussed.
